Who We Are ?
  • Welcome to EYH "Expand Your Hands", We are a small community of people most of us are from colleagues, friends and family.
  • We are here to identify helpless, orphans, oldage and needy people then trying to help them to fulfill their basic primary needs at our best.
  • This community was started on January 01st 2013, with all of your support and valuable contribution we are still running it alive and trying to help people.
